## Built in the late 1970s by the late Jewel Goddard, a lifelong sailor who once crossed the Pacific in a Morgan Out Island 41 sailboat.

Elevated Tides is a unique waterfront destination in Portland, Oregon, offering both a floating sauna experience and a well-established moorage facility on the Columbia River. Located in the Bridgeton neighborhood, just two miles east of the I-5 Bridge, Elevated Tides provides easy access to the river without the hassle of bridge traffic. The moorage features 76 slips and side-tie berths, accommodating boats up to 45 feet in length. It welcomes sailboats, small powerboats, ski boats, fishing boats, cuddy cabin vessels, kayaks, and canoes. Amenities include 30-amp shore power, water on the docks, ample free parking, and a friendly, boater-managed staff dedicated to making the rental process straightforward and enjoyable.

The floating sauna at Elevated Tides offers a serene, wood-fired retreat with panoramic views of the Columbia River, providing guests with a tranquil space to relax and recharge.
