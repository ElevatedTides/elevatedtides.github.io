Moorage & Recreation

Affordable, deep-water moorage on the Columbia River awaits you—enjoy easy access, stunning views, and peace of mind knowing your sailboat is safely docked in one of Portland’s most convenient and boater-friendly marinas.

We offer convenient kayak, canoe, and stand-up paddleboard rentals as well as secure, easy-access storage, ensuring your gear is always ready when the water calls. Visit us for the perfect launch point for your next river adventure.
