Moorage tenant and guest parking in “Sailor Lots” only. Vehicles without a parking sticker or pass are subject to towing at the owner’s expense.

We have 76 slips and side-tie berths that accommodate vessels up to 45 feet.

While sailboats make up the majority, we also welcome small powerboats, ski boats, fishing boats, cuddy cabins, kayaks, and canoes.

A scenic row of 14 floating homes lines the marina, adding to the charm and sense of community.

We are unable to accommodate live-aboard or overnight tenants under any circumstances.
